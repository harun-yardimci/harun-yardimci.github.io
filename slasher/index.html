Slasher – Absurd Survivors

Throw a sock. Survive the apocalypse.

A fast, funny survivor-style action roguelike where your weapons make absolutely no sense — and that’s the point. Hurl socks, fling slippers, launch chickens, spill scalding tea and unleash angry pigeons on relentless monster hordes.

Download on the App Store
Free · Offline · iPhone

Why it’s absurdly fun

🧦

An arsenal that makes no sense

Socks, slippers, chickens, scalding tea, angry pigeons — ridiculous weapons, deadly results. Combine and upgrade them into an unstoppable mess.

👍

One-thumb controls

Move with one thumb; your gear auto-attacks. Easy to pick up, hard to put down, perfect for quick runs anywhere.

✈️

Fully offline

No connection needed. Play on the subway, on a plane, or wherever the horde finds you.

🗺️

Chapters & boss fights

Fight through escalating chapters packed with zombies, skeletons, slimes and werewolves — each capped by a boss that means business.

🦸

Unlockable heroes

Samurai, ninja, plague doctor and more — each hero brings a different flavor of chaos to your runs.

🎁

Daily quests & rewards

Come back for daily quests, reward chests and a growing codex of every ridiculous thing you’ve met and thrown.
